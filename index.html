The spirit of B-BBEE
legislation


As a company, the Oceanic Capital team is led by the vision of founder Adrian Appanna, who believes that B-BBEE goes far beyond the scorecard. The spirit of B-BBEE legislation defines him as a leader and is embodied in the essence of his company.





Adrian Appanna CA (SA)
Chief Executive Officer



After completing his articles at KPMG South Africa and New York, Adrian joined Nedbank as part of the project finance team. Subsequently, he became a partner at African Capital Partners. He was then appointed at Transcend Capital, where he was responsible for deal-making and structuring B-BBEE deals for large multinationals.

With this corporate finance background, Adrian is a specialist in negotiating B-BBEE transactions and structuring B-BBEE ownership transactions for corporates, both local and multinational, and private equity transactions.
